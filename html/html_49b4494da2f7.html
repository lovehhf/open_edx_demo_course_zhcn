Discussion FORUMS

The discussion forum for each course is found at the top of the course page. You might come across a subset of the discussion forum inside the course (see below), where you can talk with fellow students about the course in context.  Go ahead and be social! Make your first post in this demo course.

Keep an eye out for posts with a green check mark.  The green check  means the post has been recognized by a staff member or forum moderator as a great post. You can also actively upvote a post. Others can search on user “upvoted” posts.  They tend to be very helpful.
